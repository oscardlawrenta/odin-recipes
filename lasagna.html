Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna made with zucchini slices instead of pasta.

This low-carb, gluten-free beef lasagna is delicious and satisfying; it's a crowd-pleaser!

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • ½ large zucchinis, thinly sliced lengthwise
  • tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 ½ cups low-carb marinara sauce
  • 2 teaspoons salt, divided
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (8 ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • aluminum foil
